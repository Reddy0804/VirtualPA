About BIntellect

Abstract:

Online Application for the facilitation of Entrepreneurs Business statistics using Generative AI.

Nowadays, there is rapid growth in the field of Entrepreneurship where all the young stars are willing to build their own businesses and communities. Moreover, every person in Entrepreneurship will have to manage business models, statistics, work & cash flow, manufacture, profit & loss calculations, capital budgeting, and many more.

The above-mentioned constraints are not available in one place. Different aspects are in different domains, like payments being online or offline, third-party vendor information in spreadsheets, and profit & capital investments being managed mentally. This leads to unsuccessful business management and failure of business models due to the lack of detailed information.

To overcome this situation, we came up with the idea of the "online application for the facilitation of entrepreneurs Business statistics using Generative AI." The application enables entrepreneurs to overcome confusions over their business and helps them find all the required statistics. This application integrates day-to-day accounting, purchases, sales, returns, cash flow, stock-taking, budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis, third-party vendor management like invoicing and payment system leveraging smart payment solutions, and online smart banking eliminating manual payments and session practices. This enables all the options for entrepreneurs, and they need not worry about business planning again and again.
